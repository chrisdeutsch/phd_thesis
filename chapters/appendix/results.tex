\subsection{Nuisance Parameter Rankings in the Search for Resonant \HH
  Production}%
\label{app:rankings_resonant}
% ------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The rankings of NPs after the fit to data in all channels are shown in
\Cref{fig:ranking_pulls_mx300,fig:ranking_pulls_mx500,fig:ranking_pulls_mx1000}
for the serach for resonant \HH production with $\mX = \SI{300}{\GeV}$,
\SI{500}{\GeV}, and \SI{1000}{\GeV}. The rankings are defined in analogy to
\Cref{fig:nonres_np_rankings} except that the cross section
$\sigma(\pp \to X \to \HH)$ is used as the POI instead of the signal strength.

\vspace*{2.9em}

\begin{figure}[htbp]
  \centering

  \includegraphics[scale=1.0]{results_res/rankings/ranking_reso_300}

  \caption[Rankings of NPs in the search for resonant \HH production with
  $\mX = \SI{300}{\GeV}$.]{Rankings of NPs in the search for resonant \HH
    production with $\mX = \SI{300}{\GeV}$. The rankings are shown after the fit
    to observed data in all analysis channels. The best-fit cross section is
    $\hat{\sigma}(\pp \to X \to \HH) =
    (\num{-180}\valuesep^{\hspace{0.25pt}+\hspace{0.25pt}310}_{-320})\valuesep\si{\femto\barn}$.}%
  \label{fig:ranking_pulls_mx300}
\end{figure}


\begin{figure}[htbp]

    production with $\mX = \SI{500}{\GeV}$. The rankings are shown after the fit
    to observed data in all analysis channels. The best-fit cross section is
    $\hat{\sigma}(\pp \to X \to \HH) =
    (\num{6}\valuesep^{\hspace{0.25pt}+\hspace{0.25pt}20}_{-17})\valuesep\si{\femto\barn}$.}%
